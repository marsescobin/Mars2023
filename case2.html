How we shortened the onboarding time at Magic from weeks to minutes

Great PMs endlessly look for blockers to unblock.

They’re always on the hunt for bottlenecks, unmade decisions, and anything that is keeping their team from operating like a well-oiled machine.

Lenny Ratchitsky

In any marketplace, efficient onboarding is crucial. And this was our problem. We were registering days, sometimes weeks of onboarding time beacause of contract signing delays.

The root cause is the cumbersome, manual process of contract creation and signing. Our existing payment and compliance provider at the time, Deel, lacked a public API which meant we couldn't automate it. And we've been sitting on their private beta waitlist for a while now.

What’s a product manager to do? Wait patiently? Not quite my style. Instead, I reached out directly to Deel’s product team and brought our use case to light. To my delight, they immediately responded and we set up a productive meeting. I paired up with a counter PM from their side and in a few weeks time, streamlined the contract signing process.

Results? Inspiring:

Onboarding? Down from weeks to minutes.

Support tickets? Plummeted.

Support time? Saved 50 hours each month.
